## How to Read This Report

1. **All 🟢** = Execute your planned trade
2. **Any 🔴 or 🟡** = Wait and re-check in 2-4 hours
3. **All 🔴** = Absolutely do not trade, hold cash

The most important signal is the **divergence** in Check ②: when retail is net selling but the price remains stable, this means smart money is absorbing the selling pressure — a classic bottom signal.
