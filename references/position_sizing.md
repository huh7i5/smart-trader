# Position Sizing Guide

## Core Principles

Position sizing determines **how much** to invest in each trade. It is the single most important factor in long-term survival. A great analysis with bad position sizing will blow up your account. A mediocre analysis with good position sizing will keep you alive.

---

## The Three Pillars

### 1. Single Trade Maximum: 6-12% of Total Capital

| Risk Tolerance | % of Capital | $1K Account | $5K Account | $10K Account |
|:---|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Conservative | 6% | $60 | $300 | $600 |
| Standard | 10% | $100 | $500 | $1,000 |
| Aggressive | 12% | $120 | $600 | $1,200 |

**Recommendation**: Use 10% as your default. Drop to 6% when uncertain. Never exceed 12%.

### 2. Cash Reserve: Always Maintain 30%+

Your cash (USDT/stablecoin) balance must never drop below 30% of your total account value. This ensures you always have ammunition for opportunities.

```
Available for Trading = Total Account × 0.70
Cash Reserve = Total Account × 0.30

Example:
  Total Account: $1,776
  Available: $1,776 × 0.70 = $1,243
  Cash Reserve: $1,776 × 0.30 = $533 (minimum)
```

### 3. Batch Entry: Split into 3-5 Tranches

Never enter a full position at once. Split your allocation across multiple price levels:

**Example: $300 allocated to BTC**

| Tranche | Price | Amount | Rationale |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---|
| 1st | $65,000 (current) | $60 | Initial position to "get skin in the game" |
| 2nd | $63,000 (-3%) | $80 | First dip buy |
| 3rd | $61,000 (-6%) | $80 | Deeper correction |
| 4th | $59,000 (-9%) | $80 | Panic zone — best entry |

This averages your entry to ~$62,000 instead of $65,000, saving ~4.6%.

## DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging) Schedule

For long-term accumulation, use a fixed schedule regardless of price:

### Weekly DCA
- Invest a fixed amount every week (e.g., $50/week)
- Best for volatile assets (BTC, ETH, SOL)
- Smooths out volatility over time

### Bi-weekly DCA
- Invest every 2 weeks (aligned with paychecks)
- Good balance between frequency and effort

### Monthly DCA
- Invest once per month (e.g., on the 1st)
- Simplest to manage
- Less optimal for volatile assets

**Recommended**: Weekly DCA for crypto, monthly DCA for bStocks (tokenized US stocks).

## Rebalancing

Review and rebalance your portfolio quarterly:

1. Calculate current allocation percentages
2. Compare against your target allocation
3. If any asset deviates >5% from target, rebalance
4. Sell overweight positions, buy underweight ones
5. Ensure cash reserve is still ≥30%

**Example**:
- Target: BTC 30%, SOL 15%, Cash 30%
- Current: BTC 40%, SOL 10%, Cash 25%
- Action: Sell some BTC (overweight), buy SOL (underweight), rebuild cash to 30%
